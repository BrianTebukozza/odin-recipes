PIZZA, WHO NEVER EATS IT!?

Pizza, okay you pronounce it as Pitsa or whatever sounds good to you..

Today, we are diving into how to make that great one for your next delicious bite and family. The vegetable pizza ofcourse.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Water
  • Yeast
  • Olive oil
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Prepare the Dough: Combine flour, yeast, salt, water, and olive oil. Knead for 10-15 minutes until smooth and elastic, then let it rise in a warm place until doubled in size.
  2. Preheat the Oven and Stone: Preheat your oven to a high temperature, ideally 450°F to 500°F (230-260°C). Place a pizza stone or heavy baking sheet inside to get hot for 30-60 minutes.
  3. Prepare Sauce and Toppings: Make a simple, thick sauce using tomatoes, olive oil, garlic, and herbs. Shred mozzarella cheese and prepare desired toppings (meats, vegetables).
  4. Shape the Dough: Punch down the risen dough and divide it. On a floured surface, stretch or roll out the dough to about 1/4-inch thickness, leaving the edges slightly thicker for a crust.
  5. Assemble the Pizza: Place the dough on a pizza peel or parchment paper. Spread a thin layer of sauce, top with cheese, and add toppings sparingly to ensure a crisp crust.
  6. Bake the Pizza: Transfer the pizza to the preheated stone or baking sheet in the oven. Bake for 10-15 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is bubbly.
  7. Serve: Remove from the oven, let it rest for a few minutes, slice, and serve.
